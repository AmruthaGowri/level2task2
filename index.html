BTS Tribute Page

BTS (Bangtan Sonyeondan) is a South Korean boy band formed in Seoul in 2013. The group consists of seven members: RM, Jin, Suga, J-Hope, Jimin, V, and Jungkook.

Since their debut, BTS has become one of the most successful and popular boy bands in the world, known for their catchy songs, intricate choreography, and meaningful lyrics. They have won numerous awards and accolades, including several Billboard Music Awards and American Music Awards.

Some of their most popular songs include "Dynamite," "Blood Sweat & Tears," and "Fake Love." They have also released several albums, including "Map of the Soul: 7" and "BE."

BTS has a massive global fanbase, known as the ARMY (Adorable Representative MC for Youth), who are known for their passionate support of the group and their charitable activities. BTS is also known for their philanthropy, and have been involved in several campaigns to promote mental health awareness, education, and environmental causes.
